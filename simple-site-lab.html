About me

I am someone who enjoys photography as a way to explore and appreciate the world around me. Taking photos helps me slow down and notice small details that others might miss. Through my camera, I like to express my curiosity and creativity, especially when I am outdoors and surrounded by nature.

My main interest in photography is capturing nature, especially small subjects like mushrooms and insects. I enjoy observing shapes, colors, and textures and turning then into meaningful images. Photographing these tiny details makes me feel connected to nature and helps me see beautiful places that are often overlooked.
